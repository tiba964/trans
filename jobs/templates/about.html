The goals of AIFRD

  • The Foundation works on community awareness projects for the purpose of advancing the community and combating extremist ideas.
  • The Foundation seeks to alleviate cases of persecution and injustice against aggrieved citizens and help them restore their rights.
  • Contribute to achieving societal solidarity among all segments of society, especially minorities.
  • Work to secure the emergency response to the displaced and the displaced at any time by implementing rapid interventions in the field.
  • Working to promote and build peaceful coexistence between different nationalities, religions and sects, reject violence and sectarianism, and spread peace.
  • Spreading a culture of cooperation between society and the government to reach the highest goals of transparency.
  • Adopting renewable ideas and encouraging the youth group to follow them and work to achieve them and to achieve the vision of the institution.

OUR VISION

We aspire to a world in which the rights of children, as defined in the Convention on the Rights of the Child, are always respected. A world in which children can thrive in safe environments and become agents of change they wish to see in their lives.

OUR MISSION

Ashor Iraqi Foundation for Relief and Development, founded in 2014, is an independent, neutral and impartial Iraqi organisation committed to bringing meaningful and lasting change to the lives of children and youth, especially to those most exposed to risks. We strive to improve their well-being .
